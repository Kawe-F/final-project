My favorite Recipe, Omlette

This omelette is a simple recipe with a multitude of flavours. You can have it anytime you feel hungry.

>Ingrediants of my Omlette

  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Garlic

>How to make it

  1. Heat a pan and add crushed garlic to it
  2. Then add chopped tonatoes and let the cook
  3. Till then, slice the onions and fry them
  4. once that is done check the tomatoes and add salt
  5. Mix again
  6. Now cracks two eggs on the tomato base and let the cook
  7. once the eggs are ready, garnish them with fried oinions

Serve with a flat bread and enjoy the taste
